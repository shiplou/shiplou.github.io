Shao-Ping Lu ()

I had been an associate professor at Nankai University in Tianjin, China in 2018-2022. Before that, I was a senior researcher at Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB), as a postdoc I had been in the team of Prof. Adrian Munteanu. I received my PhD degree in 2012 from Graphics & Geometric Computing Group at Department of Computer Science in Tsinghua University in China under the supervision of Prof. Shi-Min Hu.

My research interests lie primarily in the intersection of visual computing, computer vision and computer graphics, with particular focus on 2D/multiview/3D image and video processing, computational photography and representation, visual scene analysis, machine learning and mathematical optimization.
